Emporio Armani AR11581

DESCRIPTION
The Emporio Armani AR11581 Antonio Chronograph combines timeless elegance with a touch of sporty flair, featuring a silver stainless steel case and matching bracelet. Its striking green dial adds a bold pop of colour, enhancing its contemporary appeal. Powered by a reliable quartz chronograph movement, this timepiece ensures precise timekeeping and stopwatch functionality. With a versatile 41mm case size, it offers a balanced presence on the wrist, suitable for various occasions. Designed for everyday wear, it boasts 50 meters water resistance, making it resilient against splashes and brief immersion, while maintaining its stylish charm.

  • Quartz Chronograph Movement
  • 41mm Case Size
  • Stainless Steel Case and Bracelet
  • 50 Meters Water Resistant
