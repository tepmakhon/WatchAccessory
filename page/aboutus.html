ABOUT US


Watch Depot is a trusted online store dedicated to watches for men, women and kids. It all started with our passion for watches, and a mission of providing

top brands at top deals!

We are a genuine stockist of all the top brands, so keep an eye out for names like Casio G-Shock, Baby-G, Citizen, Seiko, Tommy Hilfiger, Daniel

Wellington, and much more! The company was born from a passion and love for watches, which remains at the core of everything we do.


We are family owned and our managing director, Toby Bensimon, is a dedicated watch enthusiast and collector. You can be sure that our love for all things

watches starts at the top and trickles into every department, decision and aspect of our business.

Watch Depot operates on Kaurna country. We acknowledge the Kaurna people as the traditional custodians of the Adelaide region and pay our respects to

Kaurna elders past and present.
