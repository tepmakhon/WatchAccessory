GUESS MAJESTIC GW0796G3

DESCRIPTION
The Guess Majestic GW0796G3 Multi-Function watch epitomizes luxury and sophistication, featuring a stunning two-tone gold PVD and stainless-steel case and bracelet. Its crystal-set bezel and black dial adorned with crystal embellishments exude elegance and glamour. Powered by a quartz multifunction movement, this timepiece ensures precise timekeeping and offers additional functionalities. With a bold 48mm case size, it makes a bold statement on the wrist, perfect for those who prefer a larger watch face. Designed for style and practicality, it includes 30 meters water resistance, providing protection against splashes and light rain. Ideal for both formal occasions and everyday wear, this watch combines exquisite design with versatile functionality, adding a touch of luxury to any outfit.

  • Quartz Multifunction
  • 48mm Case Size
  • Gold PVD and Stainless-Steel Case and Bracelet
  • 30 Meters Water Resistant
