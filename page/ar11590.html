Emporio Armani World Explorer AR11590

DESCRIPTION
The Emporio Armani World Explorer AR11590 Diver GMT showcases a blend of functionality and style, featuring a silver stainless steel case and matching bracelet. Its distinctive blue and red bezel surround ing a vibrant blue dial evokes a sense o f adventure and exploration. Powered by a reliable quartz movement, this timepiece ensures accurate timekeeping and GMT functionality for tracking multiple time zones. With a subst antial 42mm case size and 22mm band width, it offers a robust presence on the wrist, complemented by a durable mineral crystal that enhances scratch resistance. Equipped with a date display at the 3 o'clock position and 100 meters water resistance, it is built for both everyday wear and underwater adventures, embodying versatility and durability.
