HUGO BOSS STRIKE 1514170

DESCRIPTION
The Hugo Boss Strike 1514170 watch blends classic design with modern functionality. Featuring a silver stainless steel case, bezel, and bracelet, it offers a timeless and durable aesthetic. The black patterned dial with silver accents adds depth and sophistication, ensuring clear visibility. Powered by precise quartz movement, it delivers reliable timekeeping accuracy, enhanced by a practical date display feature. With water resistance up to 50 meters, this timepiece is suitable for everyday wear, combining style with resilience in various environments. The Hugo Boss Strike 1514170 is an ideal accessory for both formal occasions and casual outings, offering versatility and sophistication in one elegant package.
